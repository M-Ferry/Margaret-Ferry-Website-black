A little bit about me.

I’ve always had a natural ability to connect with people and a passion for creativity. When the world shut down, I knew I needed to adapt. That's when I discovered web development and the potential to work remotely, like so many others. I quickly realized that web development is more than just a skill—it's an art form that combines connection and communication. It’s incredibly rewarding to help businesses grow and thrive through design.

Now, I’ve fully transitioned into website development, and I couldn’t be happier. My years of customer service have sharpened my organizational skills and my ability to build rapport with clients, while my keen eye for detail helps me write clean, elegant code. This career change has been fulfilling, and I know I’ve found my true passion—I wouldn’t look back.
